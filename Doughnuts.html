Donuts….delicious, often glazed, typically circular ring of fried dough that Canadians love. The concept is simple enough, but donuts have come a long way from your simple Tim Hortons honey dipped treats.

Donuts have been around since the mid19th century and have evolved with society. There are now vegan donuts (for the animal lovers), keto donuts (for the carb conscious consumers), matcha flavoured varieties (for global minded adventurists snackers) and baked not fried varieties for the somewhat health conscious. If you’re not too hungry you can get Timbits or donut holes, and if you are famished you can get donuts stuffed with a variety of other treats.

Source

The only limit to donut creations is a lack of imagination (or perhaps a lack of appreciation for a good treat). But when it comes down to it, there are really two types of donut connoisseurs, the Traditionalist, or the Non-Conformist. Which are you?
